Hello, I’m Taylor.

My home is Walnut Creek, California.

Some background information

I’m an aspiring web designer. I spend a lot of my free time doing arts and crafts and one of my Treehouse goals is to create a website that features my work. My crafting specialties are needle felting, print making, preserved floral wall hangings, and jewelry design. I first became interested in web design while I was working as a studio designer for a company called Planted Design, and my interest in web development has been growing ever since.

It's safe to say 2020 was not the best year for a lot of people. I lost my job due in large part to the Covid pandemic; but that also gave me the opportunity to sign up for Treehouse.

My goals at Treehouse

I want to be able to create a website to promote and sell my crafts. I would also like to feel comfortable enough with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript so that I can start an online portfolio in hopes of landing a great job in the future.
